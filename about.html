About Stackly

Our Story

Building Digital Excellence Since 2014

Stackly was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to help businesses thrive in the digital age. What started as a small team of passionate designers and developers has grown into a full-service digital agency serving clients across the globe.

Over the past 12 years, we've had the privilege of working with startups, SMEs, and Fortune 500 companies, delivering over 500 successful projects that have transformed businesses and created lasting digital impact.

Our approach combines creativity with data-driven strategy, ensuring every project we undertake delivers measurable results and exceeds expectations.
